Policy Overview

Please review these terms before submitting an order request.

```

Custom Orders

All cabinets are built to order based on customer specifications. Because of this, orders cannot be returned once production begins.

Order Review

All orders submitted through the website are requests only. Final details, pricing, and approval are confirmed before production starts.

Changes & Cancellations

Changes or cancellations must be made before production begins. Once materials are cut or assembled, changes may not be possible.

Quality Guarantee

We guarantee that all cabinets will be built to agreed specifications and meet standard craftsmanship quality.

Defects & Issues

If there is a defect in materials or construction, we will work with the customer to repair or replace the issue.

Customer Responsibility

Customers are responsible for providing accurate measurements and project details. Errors in provided measurements may affect the final product.
```
