About

Container Custom Cabinets began as a side hobby focused on building custom cabinetry and built-ins from a repurposed shipping container workshop. This website was created to make the business more official and usable in real-world situations, allowing customers to view work, request custom builds, and communicate clearly online.

What we build

Kitchen cabinets, bathroom vanities, mudroom lockers, shelving, entertainment centers, office built-ins, and other custom solutions.

How it works

Measure, design, build, finish, and install. Projects are handled with clear communication and realistic timelines.

Materials

Common materials include plywood cabinet boxes, hardwood face frames, and durable finishes selected based on budget and use case.
