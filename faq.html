Frequently Asked Questions

Answers to common questions about Container Custom Cabinets.

```

Are the online prices final?

No. Online prices are estimates. Final pricing is confirmed after the order request is reviewed.

Can I order custom sizes?

Yes. Customers can enter custom width, height, and depth measurements for each cabinet.

What materials are available?

Standard pricing is based on maple. Stain grade or non-maple material may increase the price.

How do I submit an order?

Build your cabinets on the home page, add them to the cart, and complete the checkout form.

Do you offer delivery?

Delivery or pickup details are reviewed after the customer submits an order request.

How long does production take?

Production time depends on the size of the order, material availability, and project details.
```
